Studio Refresh SOP Video

Created as part of a company-wide initiative to improve operational consistency across studios.

This video served as a standard operating procedure (SOP) guide for conducting a comprehensive “Studio Refresh”—a full walkthrough of a studio space, followed by issue tracking and project implementation. While many submissions were quick phone-recorded clips, I saw it as an opportunity to create something elevated.

I taught myself iMovie, learned DSLR video shooting and tripod setup, recorded voiceovers, and even included a stop-motion intro—showcasing both my adaptability and creative problem-solving. I wanted to make the guide both informative and engaging, with a lighthearted twist: my two Pomeranians starred as SoulCycle studio managers.

The result was a fully produced training video that helped our team win the company-wide competition. More than that, it demonstrated my eagerness to exceed expectations and my ability to bring a creative, thoughtful lens to operational challenges.
