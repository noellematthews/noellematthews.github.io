Studio Refresh Instructional Video

A creative internal training guide that turned into a full production I directed, shot, voiced, and edited myself.

Our Facilities Manager asked each studio team to submit a short video reminding staff how to complete a standard “Studio Refresh.” While most teams submitted simple phone recordings, I took a different approach and produced a full instructional video, despite having no prior experience with video production.

I taught myself iMovie, learned how to shoot DSLR video with a tripod, recorded voiceovers, and created a stop-motion intro with the help of a teammate who also showed me the technique. I wanted the video to be informative but also engaging. My two Pomeranians starred as SoulCycle studio managers, wearing handmade uniforms, which gave the project a playful but memorable twist.

The final result was a winning submission and a personal milestone that reflected my creativity, adaptability, and commitment to producing something beyond expectations.
