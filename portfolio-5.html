Midterm Visualization: Fast Food Sales

As part of a midterm project for my Data Visualization course, I used R to recreate and reimagine a graphic based on QSR Magazine's Top 50 Fast Food Chains of 2023. The assignment required replicating the original chart's aesthetic and structure, then designing an alternative visualization to highlight new insights. I applied geom_point(), mapped aesthetics like size and color, adjusted scales, and incorporated titles, labels, and themes—including a bonus light blue background.

One version mimics the original bubble chart; the second offers a fresh take on how fast food chains compare by average unit volume and total U.S. sales.
